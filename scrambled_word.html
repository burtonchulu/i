Scrambled word

What are scrambled words

These are words whose letters have been presented in a random manner.This is exploited for game purposes by creating games in which the player must unscramble the letters to discover the original word.

Main features of the game

From the letters of a word presented in random unscramble the word represented by the letters.For example the letters 'nfdi' can be unscrambled to give the word 'find'.
Sometimes more than one word can be represented by the same letters.An example is the case of the letters 'olfg'.In this case two words are possible namely 'golf' and 'flog'.In such a scenerio only one answer will be applicable.Scoring is influenced by the length of the words you are able to unscramble. The longer they are the higher the score you earn.

What you can benefit from the game

There are several reasons why you should play word scramble.It is the best word game because

  • it will help you improve your reading and spelling in an entertaining way
  • it will challenge your mind and keep your thinking sharp

Ages that the game targets

This game has something for all ages.Both children and adults will find it both beneficial and enjoyable. Kids can practice their spelling in a game environment while grown ups can tackle the more challenging words.

Other names of the game

There are other names that can be used to refer to this game.Possible names include word scramble,scramble words,unscramble and wordscram.
