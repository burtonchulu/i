What are scrambled words

Scrambled words are words that have been taken and and their letters re-arranged in random for the purpose of challenging the player to figure out the word represented by the randomised letters.

Benefits of playing the game

If are you looking to improve your english spelling and reading this word game will help you achieve your goal. Get served scrambled words for you to figure out.

How to play the game

There are three levels to choose from when ready to play the game. These are the easy, medium and difficult word levels. The easy level serves the easiest words to figure out while the hard level serves the most difficult words.

  • To play hit the scramble button in the virtual keyboard and you will be served a scrambled word.
  • Enter the answer and hit submit to submit your answer.
