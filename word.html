Spelling practise

Take your vocabulary to a higher level with this scrambled word game. Construct words with the correct spelling from a list of randomised letters of the word. Watch your word get updated in real time right beside the random letters before you hit the submit button.

Choose the level of difficulty of the words to solve when ready to play the scrambled word game. These are the easy, medium and difficult word levels. The easy level serves the easiest words to figure out while the hard level serves the most difficult words. This allows you to select the level of challenge most appropriate to your spelling practise needs.

  • To play hit the scramble button in the virtual keyboard and you will be served a scrambled word.
  • Enter the answer and hit submit to submit your answer.
