Word games with no ads

Word games are some of the most intellectually stimulating type of games that one can play and enjoy.Traditionally these types of games have been based on the print media as well as on boards.However with the increased ease of access to the internet one can now play games such as the crossword puzzle and scrambled words online.However the downside with the online games are the frequent ads that pop up.We have provided you with some games that have no ads for you to enjoy.

Games that you can play

  • Scrambled wordNO ADVERT In this game the system will present the player with words in which the letters making it up are presented in a random manner.The player will then have to figure out the word from the random letters.There are three levels in this game namely the easy, medium and hard levels. Starting with the easy level a beginner can progressively improve command of the english language whilst an expert can skip the easy level and tackle the harder material.
  • Missing letter word puzzle The player is shown a word with some of its letters missing. The number of the missing letters will depend on the length of the word.The longer the it is ,the more the missing letters.The player must then try to figure out the missing letters thus figuring out the whole word.To get more challenging words the player must first complete the easy level to progress through to the harder levels of the game.
  • Text twist The goal is to build as many words as possible of any length from a set of random letters before time runs out.The score depends on word length.
  • True or false quiz With this game you will test your knowledge of topics ranging from music to religion and everything in between.The goal is to correctly answer as many quiz questions as possible before the time runs out.
